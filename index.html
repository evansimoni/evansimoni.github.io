Evan Simoni

Product designer

I was hatched under the southern California sun, in a desert sanctuary called San Diego. After eighteen years in the nest, I spread my wings and touched down at UC Santa Barbara, where I studied Sociology, French and Anthropology. I learned a few things about partying, too.

Next, I soared up the coast to make my perch in the hills of San Francisco. Restlessness launched me on a circuitous quest for a satisfying career – one that would enable me to learn and grow for years to come. I fluttered through a web development bootcamp, swooped into unemployment, and eventually landed on my calling, in product design.

I’ve since migrated northeast, to Ottawa, Ontario, where I’m doing my part to make commerce better for everyone with the incredible people at Shopi﻿fy.
