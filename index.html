Evan Simoni

User Experience & Product Designer

My name is Evan, and I design software for the web and mobile devices.

I take a pragmatic approach to product challenges, and make decisions with customer perspectives in mind. I'm also a seasoned communicator who can consider multiple stakeholder interests at the same time.

Great design doesn't happen in a vacuum. That's why I learned to code at Dev Bootcamp's collaborative programming school last year. The best products are built when design and development understand each other and are working together. What good is a gorgeous interface that doesn't meet technical requirements?

Away from the computer I can be found in San Francisco swimming, enjoying live music or sipping on craft beer.
