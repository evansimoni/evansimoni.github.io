I was hatched and raised under the southern Cali﻿fornia sun, in the desert sanctuary called San Diego. After growing to nearly my full-size, I spread my wings and touched down near the beach at UC Santa Barbara, where I studied sociology, french and anthropology. I competed in swim races and grew fond of partying, too.

Next, I soared up the coast to perch in the hills of San Francisco. Restlessness launched me in pursuit of a satisfying career – one that would encourage me to learn and grow for years to come. I fluttered through a web development bootcamp, swooped into unemployment, and eventually landed on my calling, in product design.

I’ve since migrated northeast, to Ottawa, Ontario, where I’m doing my part to make commerce better for everyone with the incredible people at Shopi﻿fy.
